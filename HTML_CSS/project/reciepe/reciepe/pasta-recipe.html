Pasta Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb of pasta
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 cup of cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/4 cup of olive oil
  • 1 tsp of dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Cook the pasta according to the package instructions.
  2. In a large pan, heat the olive oil and sauté the garlic until fragrant.
  3. Add the cherry tomatoes and oregano. Cook for 5 minutes.
  4. Drain the cooked pasta and add it to the pan. Toss to combine.
  5. Serve hot with grated Parmesan cheese and fresh basil leaves.
