Biryani Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups of basmati rice
  • 1 lb of chicken, cut into pieces
  • 1 large onion, sliced
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1/4 cup of yogurt
  • 2 tbsp of biryani masala
  • 1 tsp of turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp of red chili powder
  • 1/4 cup of chopped cilantro
  • 1/4 cup of chopped mint leaves
  • 2 tbsp of ghee or oil
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. Rinse the basmati rice and soak it in water for 30 minutes.
  2. In a large pot, heat ghee or oil and sauté the sliced onions until golden brown.
  3. Add the chicken pieces and cook until they are browned on all sides.
  4. Add the chopped tomatoes, yogurt, biryani masala, turmeric powder, red chili powder, and salt. Cook until the chicken is fully cooked and the oil starts to separate from the masala.
  5. In a separate pot, bring water to a boil and add salt. Drain the soaked rice and add it to the boiling water. Cook until the rice is 70% cooked, then drain the water.
  6. Layer the partially cooked rice over the chicken mixture in the pot. Sprinkle chopped cilantro and mint leaves on top.
  7. Cover the pot with a tight-fitting lid and cook on low heat for 20-25 minutes until the rice is fully cooked and has absorbed all the flavors.
  8. Serve hot with raita or salad.
